# Master IR Library: Naim-Topping-B&O Hybrid

### ⚠️ Port Mapping Requirements
*   **Ports 1 & 2 (38kHz):** Standard IR emitters for Topping D90/A90.
*   **Port 3 (455kHz):** High-Frequency Blaster for Beomaster 8000. **Must be set to "IR Blaster" in iTach settings.**
